# Business Travel Skill

## When to Apply
Auto-load this skill when the user mentions: "business trip", "conference", "work travel", "meeting", "client visit", "offsite".

## Search Preferences
- Prefer direct/nonstop flights (time > cost for business)
- Look for refundable or changeable fares
- Prefer hotels within 1 mile of the meeting location
- Note loyalty program eligibility for expense reporting

## Itinerary Conventions
- Include buffer time before important meetings (arrive day before when possible)
- Note check-in/check-out times relative to meeting schedule
- Flag if hotel check-in might conflict with arrival time

## Budget Notes
- Business travelers often have separate expense budgets
- Ask if there is a company travel policy to follow
- Prioritize convenience over price

## Reminders to Surface
- "Would you like me to note this as a business trip for expense tracking?"
- "Should I prioritize refundable options in case the meeting changes?"
