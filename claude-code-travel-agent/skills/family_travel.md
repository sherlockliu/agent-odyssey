# Family Travel Skill

## When to Apply
Auto-load when user mentions: "kids", "children", "family", "toddler", "baby", "school break", "family vacation".

## Search Preferences
- Filter for family-friendly hotels (look for amenities: pool, kids club, connecting rooms)
- Prefer morning or early afternoon flights (avoid late nights with kids)
- Note whether hotels charge for children
- Look for suite or multi-room options

## Activity Suggestions
When searching activities, also surface:
- Age-appropriate museums (natural history, science, children's museums)
- Parks and outdoor activities
- Shows or performances suitable for families
- Restaurants with kids menus

## Budget Notes
- Count all passengers (including children) in flight estimates
- Some hotels are free for children under a certain age — note this
- Family passes for attractions can save significantly

## Reminders to Surface
- "How old are the children? This affects activity recommendations."
- "Would you like me to look for connecting rooms or suites?"
- "Some airlines have priority boarding for families — shall I note this?"
